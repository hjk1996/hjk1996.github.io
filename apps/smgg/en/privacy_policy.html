"20Q" (hereinafter referred to as the "Company") complies with the personal information protection regulations required by related laws for ICT service providers and has established a personal information processing policy based on related laws to do its best to protect users' rights. The company has made this personal information processing policy publicly available within the game, making it easy for members to view. This policy may be changed from time to time due to changes in related laws and guidelines or changes in the company's internal policies. When the company revises its personal information processing policy, it will notify the reasons and contents of the changes within the game.

Article 1 (Types of Personal Information Collected and Collection Method)

  1. Types of personal information collected
    • For member registration and various service provisions, the following personal information is collected:
      • Email address, nickname, game and service usage records, access logs
    • When using a service based on an external platform, the following information may be collected:
      • Google: Member identifier information, profile picture, profile name
    • The email address can be processed for handling customer inquiries.
  2. Personal information collection method
    • Collected through the consent procedure when signing up for the company's services
    • Automatically collected through platforms affiliated with the company and service provision
    • Collected during service registration and use, during customer interaction, either through voluntary provision by the user or by request as necessary
  3. Refusal of collection and use of personal information
    • Users can refuse the collection and use of the above personal information. However, if you refuse the collection and use of personal information, it may be difficult to use some or all of the services.

Article 2 (Purpose of Collection and Use of Personal Information)

  • The company does not disclose personal information collected without the prior consent of the user, and the collected information is not used for purposes other than the following:
    1. Member protection
      • Identity verification for member-only service usage, personal identification, prevention of malicious members (members restricted or terminated due to terms of use violations) from unauthorized use, prevention of unauthorized use, confirmation of membership intent, restriction of membership and number of sign-ups, preservation of records for dispute resolution, complaint handling, handling of grievances, and communication of notifications.

Article 3 (Retention and Usage Period of Personal Information)

  • The "Company" can take necessary measures such as terminating the contract, destroying personal information, and separately storing personal information to protect the personal information of members (referred to as "dormant accounts") who have not used the service for a consecutive year, in accordance with the "Personal Information Protection Act" and its enforcement decree. In this case, the member will be notified of the expiration date of the personal information retention period and the content of the personal information measures 30 days before the action date.
  • Personal information collected with the consent of the user is retained and used as long as the membership status is maintained, and in principle, once the purpose of collecting and using personal information is achieved, the information is immediately destroyed. However, to prevent unwanted member withdrawal due to personal information theft, personal information is retained for 7 days after the member withdrawal request.
  • Additionally, the information below is retained for the specified period for the following reasons and is never used for any other purpose.
    1. Information retention reason based on company policy
      • Record of misuse, information confirming duplicate registration
        • Reason for preservation: Prevention of misuse
        • Retention period: 1 year
    2. Information retention reason based on relevant laws
      • Records related to contracts or withdrawal of subscriptions
        • Reason for preservation: Consumer Protection in E-commerce, etc. Act
        • Retention period: 5 years
      • Records related to consumer complaints or dispute resolution
        • Reason for preservation: Consumer Protection in E-commerce, etc. Act
        • Retention period: 3 years
      • Records related to identity verification
        • Reason for preservation: Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization and Information Protection, etc.
        • Retention period: 6 months
      • Service visit records
        • Reason for preservation: Communication Secrecy Protection Act
        • Retention period: 3 months

Article 4 (Procedures and Methods for Personal Information Destruction)

  • In principle, the personal information of users is destroyed without delay when the purpose of collection and use is achieved. The company's personal information destruction procedures and methods are as follows.
  • Destruction Procedure
    • The information that users enter for purposes such as membership registration is moved to a separate DB after its purpose has been achieved and is destroyed after being stored for a certain period according to internal policies and other relevant laws.
    • Unless required by law, this personal information is not used for any purpose other than retention.
  • Destruction Method
    • Personal information stored in electronic file format is deleted using technical methods that make the record unrecoverable.
